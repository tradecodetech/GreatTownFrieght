Full Truckload

Full Truckload (FTL)

When your shipment requires an entire trailer, Full Truckload is the most efficient and direct solution. Great Town Freight provides dedicated FTL capacity across our nationwide lane network, ensuring your freight moves from origin to destination without stops, transfers, or delays.

FTL is the preferred solution for high-volume shippers, time-sensitive loads, and freight that requires the security and integrity of a dedicated trailer throughout transit.

  • Dedicated trailer capacity — no shared loads
  • Direct origin-to-destination routing
  • Competitive per-mile pricing for full loads
  • Available for dry van, flatbed, and refrigerated freight
  • Flexible scheduling to match your production and delivery windows
  • Full cargo insurance coverage on every shipment

Less Than Truckload

Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Less-than-truckload freight solutions allow shippers to pay only for the trailer space they use. Great Town Freight's LTL services provide cost-effective transportation for smaller shipments that don't require a full truck, without compromising on reliability, care, or professionalism.

Our LTL coordination ensures your freight is handled correctly, tracked throughout transit, and delivered on schedule — regardless of shipment size.

  • Pay only for the space your freight requires
  • Consolidated shipments handled with care
  • Cost-effective for 1–10 pallet shipments
  • Professional freight handling and packaging requirements
  • Tracking and documentation throughout transit
  • Competitive LTL rates across all major markets
