Drive With Great Town Freight

Professional driving opportunities with competitive pay, consistent freight, and a dispatch team that treats drivers with the respect they deserve. Company drivers and owner operators welcome.

Why Drivers Choose Great Town Freight

We built our operations around the understanding that professional drivers are the backbone of this company. When our drivers succeed, our business succeeds. That philosophy drives every decision we make about pay, scheduling, routes, and support.

Whether you're a company driver looking for consistent lanes or an owner operator seeking a professional dispatch partner, Great Town Freight provides the load volume, communication, and operational support to build a sustainable, profitable driving career.

  • Competitive Pay Rates Competitive per-mile rates for company drivers. Owner operators receive strong gross revenue on every load with transparent rate structures.
  • Consistent Freight Volume Established shipper and broker relationships provide steady load availability across our lane network — no sitting and waiting for freight.
  • Professional Dispatch Support 24/7 dispatch team that communicates clearly, handles issues proactively, and treats every driver as a valued professional — not a number.
  • Established Lane Network Preferred routes and recurring lanes mean predictable miles, familiar delivery points, and reduced deadhead on your runs.
  • Prompt, Reliable Payments Company drivers paid on schedule. Owner operators: factoring arrangements available for fast pay on every load delivered.
  • Safe, Compliant Operations We run legal, compliant operations — no pressure to violate HOS, skip inspections, or operate unsafe equipment. Ever.

Driver Requirements

We maintain professional standards for all drivers to ensure safe operations, regulatory compliance, and a quality experience for our clients and partners.

Minimum Requirements

  • Valid Class A Commercial Driver's License (CDL)
  • Current DOT Medical Examiner's Certificate
  • Clean Motor Vehicle Record (MVR)
  • No DUI/DWI convictions in the past 10 years
  • Pass pre-employment drug and alcohol screen
  • Minimum 1 year verifiable CDL driving experience
  • No serious traffic violations within past 3 years

Owner Operator Requirements

  • All company driver requirements above
  • Own or leased tractor in good mechanical condition
  • Active bobtail and occupational accident insurance
  • ELD-equipped (or lease through us)
  • IFTA registration current
  • DOT operating authority or willing to lease-on
  • Professional, customer-service oriented demeanor
