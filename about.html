About Great Town Freight

A professional freight transportation and logistics company built on reliability, compliance, and a commitment to delivering results for every client we serve.

Who We Are

Great Town Freight is a professional freight transportation and logistics company providing carrier services across the contiguous United States. We specialize in full truckload, less-than-truckload, dedicated freight, and expedited shipping solutions for businesses, shippers, and freight brokers.

Our operations are structured around three core principles: regulatory compliance, professional service, and dependable delivery. Every aspect of our business — from driver qualification to equipment maintenance to cargo handling — reflects our commitment to operating at the highest professional standard in the industry.

We serve shippers, freight brokers, and third-party logistics providers who require consistent capacity, professional communication, and a carrier they can trust to execute on every load, every time.

Commitment to Safety

Safety is the cornerstone of Great Town Freight's operations. Our safety program exceeds minimum regulatory requirements and is designed to protect our drivers, cargo, and the communities we operate in.

Driver Safety Standards

All drivers are thoroughly screened with background checks, MVR reviews, drug and alcohol testing, and must meet all FMCSA qualification standards before operating any vehicle.

ELD Compliance

Electronic Logging Devices installed across our fleet ensure full Hours of Service compliance, real-time tracking, and complete transparency in driver activity records.

Equipment Maintenance

Regular pre-trip inspections, scheduled preventive maintenance, and annual DOT inspections keep our fleet roadworthy and operating at peak safety standards.

Insurance Coverage

Comprehensive cargo and liability insurance coverage protects every shipment we handle. Certificates of insurance are available on request for any shipper or broker.

Ongoing Training

Continuous driver safety training, regulatory updates, and performance reviews ensure our team remains current with all industry standards and best practices.

Safety Performance Monitoring

We actively monitor FMCSA BASIC scores, CSA data, and internal safety metrics to identify areas for improvement and maintain a strong safety record.
