Regulatory Compliance & Certifications

Great Town Freight maintains all required federal and state registrations, authorities, and compliance certifications necessary to operate as a professional interstate carrier.

USDOT Number

Active USDOT registration maintained with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Administration. DOT number available upon request for broker and shipper qualification.

FMCSA Operating Authority

Active MC (Motor Carrier) authority issued by FMCSA. Authority maintained in good standing with no revocations, suspensions, or compliance orders.

Insurance Filings

All required insurance filings submitted and active with FMCSA — cargo, auto liability, and general liability coverage at or above federal minimums.

ELD Mandate Compliance

Full compliance with the FMCSA Electronic Logging Device mandate. All commercial vehicles equipped with certified, FMCSA-registered ELD devices.

Drug & Alcohol Program

DOT-compliant drug and alcohol testing program administered through a certified Third-Party Administrator (TPA). Consortium enrollment maintained.

Vehicle Maintenance Program

Systematic preventive maintenance program in compliance with FMCSA 49 CFR Part 396. All inspection and maintenance records retained per federal requirements.

Operational Safety Standards

Our safety program goes beyond regulatory compliance — it reflects a culture of responsibility built into every aspect of Great Town Freight's operations.

Driver Qualification

All drivers undergo full FMCSA 49 CFR Part 391 qualification — including background checks, MVR review, road testing, employment verification, and medical certification.

Electronic Logging Devices

ELD devices installed on all commercial vehicles provide real-time Hours of Service (HOS) tracking, eliminating manual log violations and ensuring complete HOS compliance.

Pre-Trip Inspections

Mandatory driver vehicle inspection reports (DVIRs) required before every dispatch. All identified defects documented and repaired before the vehicle returns to service.

Drug & Alcohol Testing

Comprehensive DOT drug and alcohol testing program — pre-employment, random, post-accident, return-to-duty, and reasonable suspicion. Zero tolerance policy strictly enforced.

Ongoing Safety Training

Regular safety meetings, defensive driving programs, regulatory update training, and performance reviews keep our drivers informed and operating at peak safety standards.

CSA Score Monitoring

We actively monitor our FMCSA Compliance, Safety, Accountability (CSA) BASIC scores and take immediate corrective action to address any areas of concern or improvement.
