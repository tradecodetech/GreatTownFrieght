Professional Driver Standards

The people behind the wheel are as important as the equipment they operate. Great Town Freight maintains rigorous driver qualification standards that exceed minimum FMCSA requirements.

CDL Qualification

All drivers hold valid Commercial Driver's Licenses appropriate for the equipment they operate. CDL endorsements verified and maintained on file.

Background & MVR Checks

Thorough criminal background checks and Motor Vehicle Record reviews conducted on all drivers prior to hire and monitored on an ongoing basis.

Drug & Alcohol Testing

Pre-employment, random, post-accident, and reasonable suspicion drug and alcohol testing per 49 CFR Part 382 — zero tolerance policy enforced.

DOT Medical Certification

Current DOT medical examiner certificates required for all drivers. Medical cards tracked and renewal scheduled proactively before expiration.

Safety Training

Ongoing safety education, defensive driving training, and regulatory update programs keep our drivers informed and operating at the highest standard.

DQ File Compliance

Complete Driver Qualification files maintained per FMCSA 49 CFR Part 391 requirements — fully documented and audit-ready at all times.
